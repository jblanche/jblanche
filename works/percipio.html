Percipio

Between 2016 and 2018 I worked for Skillsoft to help them create the Percipio learning platform.

When I joined the team, they just started creating the Percipio webapp so that their customers will have a brand new learning platform to their employees. The design choices had already been made (Node.js and React.js for what I was concerned with) but were completely matching my desires at that time.

It was my first professional experience with React.js but I quickly got addicted to the library and the component approach I was already trying to pursue at Google and La Ruche Qui Dit Oui. I also likes the Backend for Frontend approach that was made in Node.js to aggregate and filter calls to lower APIs.
